Peanut Butter Cake

Description:

Simple, healthy, minimal ingredient protein cake made with chickpeas and nut butter.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chickpeas
  • 3/4 cup oats OR 3/4 cup plain rice chex cereal
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup honey OR 1/2 cup softened dates
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda. additional baking powder optional
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter, or nut butter of choice

Steps:

  1. Set oven to 350F
  2. Blend dry ingredients until fine in food processor
  3. Add all wet ingredients into the food processor. Mix until fully combined
  4. Pour into a cake or loaf pan of desired shape and depth
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes
  6. Let cool until ready to cut, then store
