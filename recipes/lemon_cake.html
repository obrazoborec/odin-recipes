Lemon Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 lemon
  • 1 3/4 cups oats
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2/3 - 3/4 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 4 dates
  • 1/2 cup plain rice chex cereal
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda

Steps:

  1. Set oven to 350F
  2. Soften dates in water by microwaving
  3. Using food processor, blend the oats and baking soda until fine
  4. Juice lemon
  5. Add all wet ingredients into the food processor (with the oat flour). Mix until fully combined
  6. Pour into a cake or loaf pan of desired shape and depth
  7. Bake for 15 minutes
  8. Let cool until ready to cut, then store
